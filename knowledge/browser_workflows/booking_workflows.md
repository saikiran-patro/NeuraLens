# Booking Workflow Checklist

## Define constraints

Record dates, location, party size, flexibility, and maximum total budget before comparing options. Keep mandatory constraints separate from preferences.

## Compare complete prices

Use the final price including fees and taxes when available. Check cancellation rules, change penalties, time zones, baggage or room inclusions, and payment timing. Do not rank options from headline price alone.

## Verify identities and dates

Names must match required identity documents. Check departure and arrival dates, overnight changes, local time, and destination. Highlight uncertainty rather than guessing.

## Payment boundary

NeuraLens can organize the comparison and navigate an approved demo, but it must not enter payment data or complete a purchase. The user reviews and submits the final transaction.
