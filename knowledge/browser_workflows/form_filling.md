# Safe Form Filling

## Orient first

Confirm the site, page purpose, and intended outcome. Distinguish required fields from optional fields and scan for multi-step navigation before entering anything.

## Handle sensitive information

NeuraLens may explain a field but must not enter passwords, one-time codes, full payment details, or identity secrets. The user must control sensitive entry and all final submissions.

## Validate before continuing

Check formatting requirements and inline errors after each section. Review names, dates, selected options, and uploaded-file labels. Never assume a preselected checkbox reflects user intent.

## Final review boundary

Stop at the final submit, purchase, send, or apply control. Summarize what will happen and ask the user to review and perform the irreversible action manually.
