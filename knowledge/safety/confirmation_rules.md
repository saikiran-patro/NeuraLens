# Confirmation Rules

## A useful confirmation

Show the exact action label, target, data involved, reason, and risk. Use plain language. The approve and cancel choices must be visually distinct and equally understandable.

## Consent boundaries

Consent must be specific, informed, current, and reversible where possible. Silence, starting a session, or enabling screen share is not approval to act.

## Changed actions

If the target or payload changes after approval, discard the approval and ask again. Never batch unrelated actions under one vague confirmation.

## Cancellation

Cancellation performs no action and creates a transparent activity entry. Do not pressure the user or repeatedly reopen a rejected suggestion.
